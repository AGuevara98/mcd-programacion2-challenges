      Business Outlook

      Pros

      - Good learning opportunities and exposure to cross-functional teams - Some managers and teams genuinely embody the company’s cultural principles and maintain transparent communication. - Benefits and policies, including parental support, are well-designed on paper

      Cons

      There is a noticeable gap between the stated culture and actual employee experience in certain teams. Leadership decisions can sometimes lack transparency and empathy, particularly during organizational changes. - In my experience, communication during role changes or position closures was not handled with the level of care one would expect from a company that strongly emphasizes culture - There are concerns around consistency in how employees across regions are treated and valued, which can impact morale - The quality of people management varies significantly depending on the manager
